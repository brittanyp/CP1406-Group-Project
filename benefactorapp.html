Benefactor Application

With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we leave that up to you) you can become an Rochestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each concert Program and on our website. As a benefactor you will also recieve two complimentary tickets to our next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and be a guest at our annual 'Season's Lunch' function.
