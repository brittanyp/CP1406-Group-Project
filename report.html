Mission

The goal of this website is to increase ticket sales of Barrier Reef Orchestra's events. The site is important as it gives information about the Orchestra and focuses on getting visiters to buy tickets. With an increase in sales the Orchestra will have more money to put towards their community to increase the quality of their performances.
